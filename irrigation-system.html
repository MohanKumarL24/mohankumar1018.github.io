Automatic Irrigation System

2025

The Automatic Irrigation System is a smart agriculture solution using ESP32, soil moisture sensors, temperature sensors, and an ultrasonic water level sensor. It automatically controls a submersible water pump based on real-time soil moisture data and water level feedback, thereby preventing under- or over-watering.

The entire system is connected to the Blynk IoT platform, allowing farmers to monitor sensor data and pump status remotely. The project contributes to water conservation and improves crop health by ensuring optimal watering intervals based on live environmental data.
