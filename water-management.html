IoT-Based Smart Water Management System

Mar 2025 – Apr 2025

Implemented a smart water level monitoring system using an ESP32 and ultrasonic sensor to track water tank levels in real time. The system automatically activates a submersible pump when the tank reaches a critical level.

Integrated with the Blynk IoT platform to enable remote monitoring and control through a mobile app, enhancing water usage efficiency and helping prevent both overflow and depletion.
