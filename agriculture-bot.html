Remote-Controlled Agriculture Bot

2025

This ESP32-based robot is designed to assist farmers in fieldwork operations such as ploughing, seed sowing, and monitoring soil moisture. The bot features remote control functionality through Bluetooth or Wi-Fi, allowing it to be operated manually over long distances. It incorporates an MG996R servo motor for ploughing, a seed/fertilizer dispensing system powered by MG92 servo, and real-time feedback from soil sensors.

The robot is compact and performs well on rough terrain. Future improvements may include GPS navigation and solar power integration.
