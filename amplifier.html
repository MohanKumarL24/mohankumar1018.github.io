Audio Amplifier Circuit with PCB

Feb 2024 – Apr 2025

Developed a compact and efficient audio amplifier circuit using the LM386 IC, capable of amplifying low-level audio signals by up to 20 times while keeping noise levels minimal. The amplifier is suitable for portable speaker projects and basic audio experiments.

A custom PCB layout was designed, fabricated, and tested to ensure reliable performance in embedded audio systems. The PCB helped reduce wiring clutter and improved the overall signal integrity.
